Our Technology and Mission

MediCore AI Inc. is dedicated to transforming mental health care through precision neurotechnology. Our platform integrates artificial intelligence with multimodal data—including neuroimaging, behavioral metrics, and clinical assessments—to identify the right patients for the right interventions at the right time. By leveraging advanced machine learning and personalized modeling, we aim to optimize non-invasive brain stimulation therapies such as transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS). Our mission is to accelerate the translation of neuroscience and AI research into scalable, accessible treatments that improve outcomes for individuals facing depression and other neuropsychiatric conditions.
